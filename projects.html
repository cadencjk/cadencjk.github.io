PollEV Notifier

Get notified for your quizzes.

Poll Everywhere (PollEV) is a web-based student response system (SRS) that works on any Internet enabled device. It is used by renowned universities worldwide, such as Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Standford University and National University of Singapore. This Chrome extension alerts the students whenever their professors release an online quiz.
